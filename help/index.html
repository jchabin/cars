What is this?
This is my incredible Online Racing Game. It's a multiplayer game where you try to complete three laps before the other players. I built it with three.js and Firebase.
How do I play?
First, type in your name and use the color slider to choose the color of your car. If you want to be in charge of the game, or you want to play alone, click
Host a Game
If someone else is hosting the game, press
Join a Game
When hosting, have all other players type in your four-letter code to join. If someone else is hosting, type in the code on their screen.

If you're on a computer, use the left and right arrow keys to steer. Your car will automatically drive forwards. On a phone, with or without VR mode, tilt your phone to steer.

Be careful not to hit other players!
